# Frequently Asked Questions

## Do I need an appointment?

Appointments are recommended for all visits. Walk-ins may be accepted for urgent cases, but availability is not guaranteed.

## What animals do you treat?

We treat dogs and cats only.

## Do you accept pet insurance?

Yes, we accept most major pet insurance providers. Payment is required at the time of service, and insurance claims are handled by the pet owner.

## How can I request a prescription refill?

Prescription refills can be requested by phone during opening hours. Some medications require a recent examination.

## Do you handle emergencies?

We can assist with urgent cases during opening hours. We do not provide emergency care outside of opening hours.

## How long does a typical visit take?

Most appointments last between 20 and 30 minutes.
